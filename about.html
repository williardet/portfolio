ABOUT US

Since 2015, we have been harnessing the force of creativity to help our clients make an incredible impact on present and future generations.

Our history is a testament to our relentless pursuit of branding and marketing excellence. Established in 2015 as "Novex" our founder, a visionary marketer with a background in public administration, set out to redefine organizational success through powerful creative.
In 2019, we rebranded as IconiQ Creative Group and expanded into a fully remote global team. With our headquarters nestled in vibrant New York City, we draw inspiration from its dynamic energy and diverse culture, infusing it into every project we undertake. Over the years, our expertise in brand identity, strategy, website design and digital marketing has flourished , forging invaluable partnerships with diverse clients across various sectors globally.
